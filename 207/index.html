Linear Transformations

T:V→WT: V \rightarrow WT:V→W

TTT is a map where VVV is the domain and WWW is the codomain. Linear transformations are special types of maps.

Definition: A linear transformation is a function T:V→WT: V \rightarrow WT:V→W with the properties that TTT:

img(T)={Tv⃗:v⃗∈V}. img(T) = \{T\vec{v}:\vec{v}\in V\}. img(T)={Tv:v∈V}.

ker(T)={Tv⃗=0⃗:v⃗∈V}. ker(T) = \{T\vec{v}=\vec{0}:\vec{v}\in V\}. ker(T)={Tv=0:v∈V}.
